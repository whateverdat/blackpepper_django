MANUEL

RECIPES

Recipes are the type of content in BlackPepper. Each consist of 4 compulsory: Title, Description, List of Ingredients and Directions; and 2 optional fields: Image and Additional Notes.

Title, Description and Image are used to display your recipe outside of recipe page itself (on homepage, search results, profile page etc.) List of Ingredients, Directions and Additional Notes are the main content of a recipe, they provide the reader with the neccessary information to cook the dish, with additional information, tips and tricks.

CREATE RECIPE

To create a recipe, you must be logged in, then access the recipe creation page by clicking 'Create' button located in the navigation bar:



After all the fields that are tagged with are filled, the Submit and Preview buttons will become available at the bottom of the page:

EDIT RECIPE

To edit your existing recipe, you must be logged in to your account that is the author of the recipe and scroll to the bottom of the page to find the 'Edit' button:

DELETE RECIPE

In a similar proces to edit your recipe, you can click the 'Delete' button, located next to the Edit button.

LISTS

A List is a way of grouping and organizing recipes. Whether yours or other people's, you can combine and create a list for your personal use. You can access your recipes from the navigation bar's 'Lists' link:


You can add recipes to your 'Lists' by visiting the page of the recipe you want to add to your list, then clicking on just below the recipe-author information.

CREATE LISTS

You can create 'Lists' by clicking to 'Create a list' button located on 'Lists' page and selecting a name for your list.


Alternatively, you can create a new 'List' directly from any recipe page. Just select 'Create New List' option while adding a recipe your lists.

EDIT LISTS

You can edit your lists from your 'Lists' page, by clicking the 'Edit' button that belongs to the list you want to edit:


From this page, you can rename your list or remove recipes from it.

DELETE LISTS

From the page to edit a specific list, you can choose to delete a list altogether, by simply clicking the 'Delete' button, located at the bottom of the page:

FOLLOWING/WATCHLIST

You can add the creators who's recipes you like to your 'Watchlist', which is essentially 'Following' them. You can add any user to your list by visiting their profile and clicking the 'Watch' button, located just below their name and profile picture:


Once the user is added to your 'Watchlist' they will show up on your 'Watching' page, accessed from navigation bar:


Additionally, their latest recipes will show up on your homepage, under the title 'Latest From Your Favorite Cooks':

PROFILE CUSTOMIZATION

Users on BlackPepper can set pictures to be displayed on their profile, and set a 'Bio' that introduces them to the community! Your 'Profile Picture' will be displayed on the page of your recipes and in search results, along with your bio!

BIO

You see your bio on your profile, above your profile information. By clicking the 'Change' button located below your bio, you can set a brief introduction:


By submitting the bio field as empty, you can 'Remove' your current bio and set it as empty:

PROFILE PICTURE

Your 'Profile Picture' is displayed in your recipes, comments and show up in search results. You can set it by heading over to your profile and clicking first on the 'Options' button under your profile picture, then on 'Change Profile Picture' button:


In a similar fashion to bios, you can 'Remove' your profile picture by clicking the 'Remove' button, prior to uploading any file.

IMAGE UPLOADS

You can upload images alongside your recipes, which is recommended! Select a file from the 'Image to Display' section while creating a recipe:


You can later change this image via 'Edit Recipe' option located on the bottom of the recipe page, however once you set up an image for a recipe, you cannot set the recipe image to empty!

THEME

BlackPepper has two different 'Themes', one of which is dark and the other one is bright! You can easily switch between them according to your preference or need by clicking on the 'Light Bulb' icon located in the navigation bar.
